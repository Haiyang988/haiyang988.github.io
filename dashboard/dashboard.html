更容易的数据追踪

The Gikoo MPS ( Mobile Performance Support ) is an e-learning app that helps the chain industry enterprises push the latest training materials to their vast front line employees. It tracks the employees learning progress, too. This dashboard delivers all important data in realtime through an easy-to-read and engaging interface to help employers comprehend their employees even better.

捷库移动学习是一款 e-learning 软件，帮助连锁行业企业将培训材料实时推送到一线门店员工手里。此外它还会对学员的学习情况进行追踪，并将数据通过简单易读的形式展现到管理者面前，进而帮助管理者更好的了解一线员工的学习情况。
