Gikoo Job

DURATION
APR 2016 - AUG 2016
TYPE
MOBILE APP
DUTY
PRODUCT MANAGEMENT, UX DESIGN


Gikoo Job is released to help college students and graduates find quality jobs in the chain industry in urban area. Instead of copying what exists, we ask ourselves,

“What are our user like ?”

“What do they really need while seeking for a new job ?”

“What are their concerns ?”

To answer these questions, we talk to them, again and again; And the result is Gikoo Job. It greets the users with a full-screen map decorated with opening jobs. It’s simple. It’s unique. It’s fun! A tap on the big and beautiful Brand Cards will bring up the Store Page. There comes the store phone gallery, which occupies the upper half of the screen, while available jobs stack at the lower part. These are two of the most frequently visited pages.

The transitions are crafted to a 0.01 seconds precision to convey the right mood.
