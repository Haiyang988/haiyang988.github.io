As the Gikoo Jobs’ successor, the 2nd version brings an updated map experience. The Brand-card’s size is shrunken so that the screen can display more “Brand-Pins”. A new Campaign Page is placed at the bottom of the screen. Scroll up to reveal more content. Drag down to hide. It doesn’t take noticeable space at all. Besides, since looking for a job means coming out for the interview, the title bar now also displays the current outdoor weather !

Highlight

The new design introduces a few swift micro interactions to enhance the experience, one of which is the unification of the Location Indicator and the Locate Button. The Location Indicator is enlarged to house the user’s picture, which provides a more immersive sensation. The larger indicator now also serves as the Locate Button. When the user’s location exceeds beyond the screen, the indicator will stay at the closest border of the screen and move along the edge with a popping arrow pointing to the users direction. You can try it yourself with the prototype at the beginning or via this link .

Video Demos

All of these videos are recorded on realtime with the prototype.

When a Brand-Pin is on tap, a Preview Board will jump up from the bottom of the screen with some of the most relevant information to a job seeker, including the distance, photos of the store, opening position and salary.

With a Preview Board open, the user can directly tap the other Brand-Pins to reveal the recruitment detail. Time saved!

When the user’s location slides beyond the screen, the Location Indicator stays and move along the closest edge, with an arrow pointing to the users location.

More content can be displayed when the Campaign Page is dragged upward.

Drag downward to hide the Campaign Page.
